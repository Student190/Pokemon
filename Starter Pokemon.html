Starter Pokemon can have one of three primary types:grass,fire,water.
In this selection you can know the best way to pick a starter based on the amount of strength and weakness a pokemon may have. It can be very challenging but if you see the value in that pokemon then it may be the best choice for you starting your journey.

Guide to what Starter should you Choose

  • Grass Pokemon are very weak compare to Fire & Water but is good for destroying Water, Rock, Ground
  • Fire Pokemon destroys bug types, grass types, ice types, steel type pokemon
  • Water Pokemon are very common buyt despite that are very good against fire, rock, and ground
  • Every Starter Pokemon have strength and weakness but choosing one depend on your playstyle
