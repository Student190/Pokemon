Pokemon Adapting
Pokemon live in water, caves, skys, space, volcanos, and even graveyards. Weak pokemon, strong pokemon, rare pokemon, and even legendaries are out there open waiting to be seen.
Pokemon can have a maxiumum of two types. Each pokemon has it weakness and it strengths. Ex: Fire is supereffective against steel, Weak against Water, and Resist Bug
When pokemon live in other regions adaption is very important for survival. A well known example is Vulpix chnaging from a fire type to an ice type when taking a liking to to Alola snowy mountains.
